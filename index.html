About Me

Emily is a Tucson based web designer and developer. She holds a Bachelor's degree from Green Mountain College in Fine Arts with the concentration of photography. She also holds Master' degree from Aberystwyth University in Art, where she continued her photography pursuits. Prior to exploring web development, Emily was an successful Art teacher for 15 years. She is currently working toward explanding her-skill set by taking a full- stack coding bootcamp through Trilogy at the University of Arizona. Emily is versed in HTML, CSS, Javascript and will be learning Node.js, Mongoose, React, Python, Java and several other programming languages throughout the course of the immersive, twelve week bootcamp.
